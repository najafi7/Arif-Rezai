About Me

I’m Arif Rezai — a graphic designer with over 3 years of experience creating memorable brand identities, logos, packaging, and digital visuals. I focus on clear communication through strong typography, color, and composition to help businesses stand out.
I specialize in logo & brand systems, print collateral, and UI visuals. I work primarily with Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Figma to deliver polished, production-ready designs.
If you need a new visual identity or design support for your project, I’m available for freelance and contract work — let’s create something great together.
